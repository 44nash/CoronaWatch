Corona Watch

The app is designed to allow people confirmed to have coronavirus, to mark there recent locations with a red Marker. People unconfirmed will mark there positions with a yellow is to marker Each marker only last 5 days and each marker is anonymous. Coronavirus is everyones problem, let everyone be the solution.

The more people using the app the better the app works. If you are a confirmed or an unconfirmed case, mark your position to keep those with underlining health conditions safer, those with out infection safer and essential works safer.

If you do not have the virus use the application to stay clear of the marked areas or provide extra caution to those areas in order to prevent the spread of the virus. Together we can stop Coronavirus and kill the second wave.
